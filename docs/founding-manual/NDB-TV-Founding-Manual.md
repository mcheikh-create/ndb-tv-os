## 2. Why NDB-TV Exists

**The problem:** Nouadhibou, Mauritania's economic capital, lacks a dedicated digital media platform that serves its citizens with practical, trustworthy, locally-relevant content in their own language.

**The gap:** Existing media is either national (focused on Nouakchott), international (not locally relevant), or entertainment-only with no educational or developmental mission.

**The opportunity:** A generation of young Nouadhibouis is online, hungry for knowledge, opportunity, and content that speaks to their reality. AI and digital tools are lowering the barriers to media production.

**The response:** NDB-TV — a digital-first, community-rooted media network that fills this gap.

## 4. Vision, Mission, and Values

### Vision

A digitally fluent Nouadhibou where every citizen has access to practical knowledge, health awareness, economic opportunity, and responsible AI literacy — delivered through media that speaks their language and respects their culture.

### Mission

Help people improve their lives through practical knowledge and responsible use of technology.

### Core Values

| Value | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| **Knowledge is a trust** | What we learn, we must share |
| **Truth above engagement** | Accuracy matters more than views |
| **Local first** | Nouadhibou content, Nouadhibou voices |
| **Practical utility** | Every video should help someone do something |
| **Responsible technology** | AI and digital tools used ethically |
| **Community ownership** | NDB-TV belongs to the people it serves |

## 6. Core Programs

### Zidni AI (زدني)
*"Increase me in knowledge."*

A program dedicated to AI literacy in Arabic/Hassaniya. Covers:
- AI tools and how to use them
- AI news and developments
- Practical demonstrations
- Ethics and responsible use
- Local AI applications

### Health First (صحتك أولاً)
Health awareness and prevention content.

### AI Helps Me
Real people demonstrating how AI solves real problems in their daily lives and work.

### NDB Champions
Profiles of local achievers, innovators, and change-makers from Nouadhibou.

### Opportunity Zone (منطقة الفرص)
Information about jobs, training programs, funding opportunities, and economic development.

### Voices of Nouadhibou (أصوات انواذيبو)
Citizen stories, street interviews, and community voices.

### I Love NDB (أنا أحب انواذيبو)
City pride content — heritage, culture, landmarks, belonging.

### Community Dialogues (حوارات مجتمعية)
Town halls, expert panels, and structured community conversations on local issues.

## 8. Operating Principles

1. **"لا تحتكر ما تعلمته اليوم"** — Knowledge shared is knowledge multiplied.
2. **Quality over quantity** — One excellent video is worth more than ten rushed ones.
3. **Consistency builds trust** — Regular, predictable publishing schedules.
4. **Feedback is fuel** — Community input shapes content direction.
5. **Transparency** — Open about funding, partnerships, and editorial decisions.
6. **Continuous learning** — The team grows as the channel grows.

---

## 9. Content Philosophy

### What We Make
- Practical, actionable content
- Locally relevant and produced
- Language-appropriate (Arabic, Hassaniya, with English subtitles where useful)
- Mobile-optimized (vertical format for shorts, readable text overlay)

### What We Don't Make
- Clickbait
- Misinformation
- Unverified health claims
- Content that exploits fear, anger, or division
- Politically partisan content

## 10. Language and Culture Policy

- **Primary language:** Arabic (Modern Standard for formal content, Darija/Hassaniya for conversational)
- **Secondary:** English subtitles for AI and tech content (broader reach)
- **Cultural framing:** Content must respect Islamic values, Mauritanian traditions, and local social norms
- **Inclusivity:** Content should represent the diversity of Nouadhibou's communities

---

## 11. Editorial Independence

NDB-TV maintains editorial independence from:
- Political parties and movements
- Commercial sponsors (content is not for sale)
- External influence campaigns

Sponsorship and partnership arrangements must never dictate or influence content. See `docs/governance/editorial-independence.md`.

---

## 12. AI Ethics and Responsible Technology

As a channel that covers AI extensively, NDB-TV commits to:

- **Transparency** — Always disclose when AI tools are used in production
- **Accuracy** — Verify AI-generated information before publishing
- **Human accountability** — AI assists but humans decide and take responsibility
- **Empowerment, not replacement** — AI should augment human capability, not replace it
- **Safety** — No promotion of harmful or deceptive AI use

See `docs/governance/ai-ethics.md`.

---

## 13. Health Content Standards

Health content carries special responsibility. All health content must:

- Cite verifiable sources (medical professionals, WHO, Ministry of Health)
- Clearly distinguish between established medical knowledge and emerging research
- Include disclaimer: "This content is for informational purposes only. Consult a healthcare professional for medical advice."
- Never recommend specific treatments, medications, or dosages
- Be reviewed by a health professional before publication when possible

See `docs/editorial-policy/health-content-standards.md`.

## 15. Growth Strategy

### Phase 1: Foundation (Year 1)
- Build consistent publishing cadence
- Grow to 1,000 subscribers
- Establish community correspondent network
- Weekly livestream as anchor event

### Phase 2: Expansion (Year 2–3)
- Scale to 10,000 subscribers
- Regional partnerships
- Diversify content formats (podcasts, written articles, WhatsApp broadcasts)
- Begin monetization

### Phase 3: Leadership (Year 4–5+)
- Leading digital media platform in Nouadhibou
- Self-sustaining through multiple revenue streams
- Replication model for other Mauritanian cities

## 19. The Next Five Years (2026–2030)

NDB-TV 2030 envisions the platform evolving into five interconnected platforms:

1. **Knowledge Platform** — Trusted, practical knowledge library
2. **AI Education Platform** — Making AI accessible to every Mauritanian
3. **Health Awareness Platform** — Improving community health outcomes
4. **Sports Development Platform** — Growing and celebrating local sports
5. **Community Development Platform** — Civic engagement and citizen voices

Each platform will have its own content track, audience strategy, and partnership model — all united under the NDB-TV brand and values.

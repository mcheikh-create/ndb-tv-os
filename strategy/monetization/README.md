# Monetization Strategy

**Prerequisite:** 1,000 subscribers
**Status:** Planning
**Target Activation:**

---

## Principles

1. **Monetization serves the mission, not the other way around**
2. **Transparency** — Audience always knows how we make money
3. **No compromise** — Never sacrifice content quality or integrity for revenue
4. **Multiple streams** — Don't depend on one source

---

## Revenue Streams

### Phase 1: YouTube Monetization (1,000 subs)

**Requirements:** 1,000 subscribers + 4,000 watch hours OR 10M Shorts views

**Estimated income:** $50–200/month initially (YouTube ad revenue varies significantly by region)

### Phase 2: Sponsorships (2,000+ subs)

**Model:** Local businesses and organizations sponsor content relevant to their sector

**Packages:**
- **Episode sponsor** — Mention in video + logo in description
- **Series sponsor** — Branding in series intro + sponsor acknowledgment in each episode
- **Livestream sponsor** — Logo on stream + verbal acknowledgment during breaks

**Rate guidance:**
- Episode sponsor: Negotiable based on viewership
- Series sponsor: 3x episode rate
- Livestream sponsor: 1.5x episode rate

### Phase 3: Community Support (3,000+ subs)

**Models to consider:**
- YouTube channel memberships
- Patreon or similar platform
- One-time community fund (e.g., Buy Me a Coffee)

**Value exchange:**
- Early access to content
- Behind-the-scenes content
- Community voting on topics
- Exclusive Q&A sessions

### Phase 4: Services (ongoing)

NDB-TV can offer services that align with mission:
- **AI training workshops** — Businesses and organizations
- **Content production services** — For community organizations
- **Digital literacy training** — Schools and community centers

---

## Financial Policy

- All revenue reinvested into content production for first 2 years
- Financial reports published annually
- Sponsorships disclosed in every sponsored video
- No paid product placement that isn't clearly labeled

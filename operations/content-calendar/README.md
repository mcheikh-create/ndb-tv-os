## Program Rotation

### Monthly Cadence
- **Zidni AI:** 2 episodes/month (core program — highest priority)
- **Health First:** 2 shorts + 1 long-form/month
- **AI Helps Me:** 2 episodes/month
- **NDB Champions:** 2 episodes/month
- **Opportunity Zone:** 1 episode/week
- **Voices of Nouadhibou:** 1 episode/week
- **I Love NDB:** 2 shorts/month
- **Community Dialogues:** 1 session/month

### Total Monthly Output Target
- 8 long-form videos
- 16 shorts
- 1 livestream

## Season Planning

Consider organizing content into seasons (12–13 weeks):

**Season Theme Example: "AI for Everyone"**
- Week 1: What is AI? (Zidni AI)
- Week 2: AI in healthcare (Health First crossover)
- Week 3: How a student uses AI (AI Helps Me)
- Week 4: AI entrepreneur (NDB Champions)
- ...continuing through 13 weeks

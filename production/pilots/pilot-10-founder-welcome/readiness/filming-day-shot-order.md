# NDB-TV Pilot #10 — Filming Day Shot Order

**Episode:** Welcome to NDB-TV
**Presenter:** Founder
**Target Length:** 5 minutes
**Total Setup Time:** ~45 minutes (setup) + ~30 minutes (recording)

---

## Setup Phase (45 minutes)

| Order | Task | Est. Time | Done? |
|-------|------|-----------|-------|
| 1 | Set up camera/tripod at eye level | 5 min | ☐ |
| 2 | Frame medium shot (head to mid-torso) — check headroom | 3 min | ☐ |
| 3 | Set up key light at 45° left or right, above eye level | 5 min | ☐ |
| 4 | Set up fill light or reflector on opposite side | 3 min | ☐ |
| 5 | Check background — clean, no clutter, no bright spots | 2 min | ☐ |
| 6 | Clip on microphone — position 15–20 cm from mouth | 2 min | ☐ |
| 7 | Check audio levels — record 10 seconds, play back | 3 min | ☐ |
| 8 | Check lighting — no harsh shadows, face visible | 2 min | ☐ |
| 9 | Turn off fans, AC, phone notifications | 1 min | ☐ |
| 10 | Place script near camera (below or beside lens) | 1 min | ☐ |
| 11 | Glass of water ready outside frame | 1 min | ☐ |
| 12 | Save test clip — label "pilot10_test" | 1 min | ☐ |

---

## Recording Phase (30 minutes)

| # | Shot | Duration | Content | Notes |
|---|------|----------|---------|-------|
| 1 | **Audio/Video Test** | 30s | Record 30 seconds of opening lines. Play back. Check audio + video quality. **Do not proceed until this passes.** | ✅ Must pass |
| 2 | **Take 1 — Full Script** | 5 min | Record entire script from "السلام عليكم" to "والسلام عليكم ورحمة الله". Do not stop for small mistakes. Keep going. | Main take |
| 3 | **Pause** | 30s | Deep breath. Review mental notes from Take 1. What to improve? | — |
| 4 | **Take 2 — Full Script** | 5 min | Record entire script again. Relax. Second takes are often better. | Backup take |
| 5 | **Optional: Take 3** | 5 min | Only if both takes have major issues. Don't over-record. | Optional |
| 6 | **Close-up: Closing Section** | 1 min | Record last 30 seconds again (closing + call to action) from "في الأيام الجاية..." to end. Good for editing options. | Optional backup |
| 7 | **Close-up: Call to Action** | 30s | Record just the participation invite section again — "إذا كنت معلم، شاب، حرفي..." | Optional backup |
| 8 | **B-Roll: Silent** | 30s | Record 30 seconds of silent room / background / logo on screen (for editing transitions). | Helpful for editor |
| 9 | **B-Roll: Logo card** | 10s | Hold NDB-TV logo card or graphic in frame (for intro/outro). | Helpful for editor |

**Estimated total recording time:** ~18 minutes of takes + breaks

---

## Handling Mistakes During Recording

| Situation | Action |
|-----------|--------|
| Small stumble on a word | Pause. Restart the sentence. **Do not stop recording.** Editor can cut it. |
| Major mistake (wrong section, blanked out) | Stop. Take a breath. Say "Take 2 restart" to camera. Start from nearest natural section beginning. |
| Outside interruption (phone, knock) | Stop. Handle the interruption. Say "Take 2 restart from [section]" to camera. |
| Lost place in script | Glance at script. Find your place. Restart sentence. Editor can handle. |
| Cough, sneeze | Cover briefly. Pause. Restart sentence. |

## Wrap-Up Phase (10 minutes)

| # | Task | Done? |
|---|------|-------|
| 1 | Stop recording on camera | ☐ |
| 2 | Save files with clear names: `pilot10_take1.mp4`, `pilot10_take2.mp4` | ☐ |
| 3 | Copy files to backup location (USB drive, cloud, second computer) | ☐ |
| 4 | Play back one full take on computer/phone — verify it recorded correctly | ☐ |
| 5 | Only then delete camera files if storage is needed | ☐ |
| 6 | Pack equipment | ☐ |

## Before You Leave the Filming Location

- ☐ Test clips deleted (not needed once takes are verified)
- ☐ All footage backed up to at least 2 locations
- ☐ Best take identified (mentally note which take felt best)
- ☐ Note down any sections that need special editing attention

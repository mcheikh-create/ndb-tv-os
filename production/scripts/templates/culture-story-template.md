## Sensitivity Checklist

Cultural stories require extra care. Complete this checklist:

- [ ] Story told with respect and dignity
- [ ] No stereotyping or generalization
- [ ] Subject(s) treated as partners, not subjects
- [ ] Community consent where applicable
- [ ] Historical claims cross-referenced with 2+ sources
- [ ] No cultural appropriation
- [ ] Language reflects how the community would describe itself

# Growth Strategy

## Purpose

Systematic approach to growing NDB-TV's audience, reach, and impact.

---

## Growth Phases

### Phase 0: Validation (0–100 subscribers)
- Prove content resonates
- Find format that works
- Build publishing habit

### Phase 1: Foundation (100–1,000 subscribers)
- Consistent weekly publishing
- Second program launched
- Community correspondent network initiated

### Phase 2: Scaling (1,000–10,000 subscribers)
- Multi-platform presence
- Formal sponsorship program
- Regular livestream as anchor event

### Phase 3: Leadership (10,000+ subscribers)
- Regional recognition
- Multi-platform specialization
- Self-sustaining revenue

---

## Distribution Channels

### Primary
1. **YouTube** — Long-form video repository
2. **YouTube Shorts** — Discovery engine
3. **WhatsApp** — Direct audience connection (broadcast lists, groups)
4. **Facebook** — Community building, cross-posting

### Secondary (Phase 2+)
1. **Instagram** — Shorts, stories, visual content
2. **TikTok** — Short-form discovery
3. **Twitter/X** — News and announcements

### Tertiary (Phase 3+)
1. **Podcast platforms** — Audio versions of content
2. **Website** — Searchable content hub
3. **Email newsletter** — Direct to audience

---

## Content Strategy for Growth

### SEO Optimization
- Arabic keyword research for every video
- Titles in Arabic with clear search intent
- Descriptions with relevant keywords
- Tags with Arabic and English terms

### Algorithm-Friendly Practices
- High retention (hook in first 15 seconds)
- Consistent publishing schedule
- End screens and cards to increase session time
- Playlists to organize content and increase watch time

### Viral Potential
- Shorts from every long-form video
- Controversy-free shareability
- Practical value that compels sharing
- Relatable local content with universal appeal

## Experimentation Framework

Each month, run one growth experiment:
1. **Hypothesis** — What we think will work
2. **Test** — Run for minimum 2 weeks
3. **Measure** — Compare to baseline
4. **Decide** — Keep, modify, or discard
5. **Document** — Record findings for future reference

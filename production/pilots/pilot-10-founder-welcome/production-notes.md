# NDB-TV Pilot #10 — Production Notes

**Episode:** Welcome to NDB-TV
**Presenter:** Founder
**Target Length:** 5 minutes
**Status:** Draft — awaiting approval before production

---

## 1. Filming Setup

### Location Requirements
- **Indoors** — quiet, controlled environment
- **Background:** Simple, clean, professional. Options:
  - Plain wall (deep blue or neutral color)
  - Home office/desk setup
  - Bookshelf or local motif
- **No windows behind presenter** (causes exposure problems)
- **No echo** — soft furnishings (curtains, carpet) help absorb sound

### Camera
- **Minimum:** Smartphone (1080p, 30fps) on tripod or stable surface
- **Better:** DSLR/mirrorless with 50mm equivalent lens
- **Position:** Eye level with presenter — not looking up or down
- **Distance:** ~1.5–2 meters from subject

### Framing
- **Medium shot:** Head to mid-torso
- **Headroom:** Small gap above head (~10% of frame height)
- **Look space:** Centered is fine for direct-to-camera presentation
- **Background separation:** At least 1 meter from backdrop

---

## 2. Audio

### Most Important Element
Audio quality matters more than video quality. Viewers forgive imperfect video but not bad audio.

### Requirements
- **Dedicated microphone REQUIRED.** Do not use camera/smartphone built-in mic.
- **Recommended:** Lavalier microphone (clip-on) — most affordable, good quality
- **Alternative:** USB condenser microphone on desk
- **Test:** Record 30 seconds before main take — check for background noise, clipping, hiss

### Audio Rules
- No recording in rooms with echo (bare walls, tile floors)
- Turn off fans, AC, refrigerators, phone notifications
- Distance from microphone: 15–30 cm (6–12 inches)
- Consistent volume — not too quiet, not peaking
- Leave 2 seconds of silence at start and end for editing

### Backup
- Record audio separately on phone as backup (Voice Memos app)
- Sync to video in editing

---

## 3. Lighting

### Minimum Setup
- **Key light:** Main light source at 45° to camera, slightly above eye level
- **Fill:** Reflector or second light on opposite side (less bright)
- **Natural light:** If using window light, face the window — don't have it behind you

### Lighting Rules
- No harsh shadows across face
- No bright background (presenter should be brighter than background)
- Eyes should be visible — no dark eye sockets
- Consistent lighting throughout recording

### Zero-Budget Option
- Sit facing a large window (diffused daylight) during daytime
- Place a white board or paper on the opposite side to bounce light
- Avoid mixed lighting (daylight + warm indoor light)

---

## 4. Wardrobe

### General
- Solid colors preferred. Deep blue, white, or neutral tones work well.
- Avoid fine stripes, busy patterns, or all-black/all-white.
- Modest and professional.

### Not Recommended
- T-shirts with large logos or text
- Busy patterns (cause moiré on camera)
- Hats or sunglasses
- Excessive jewelry

### Recommended
- Clean, pressed clothing
- Collared shirt or modest top
- Solid deep blue or white aligns with NDB-TV brand colors

---

## 5. Delivery

### Preparation
- Read the script aloud at least 3 times before recording
- Mark natural pauses in the script
- Practice the opening 30 seconds until it feels natural

### On-Camera Rules
- **Look at the camera lens** — not the screen, not the script
- If using a teleprompter, practice eye movement first
- Speak at a natural pace — slightly slower than conversation
- Pause between sections — let each point land
- Be yourself. Do not perform. Authenticity matters more than perfection.

### If You Make a Mistake
- Pause, take a breath, restart the sentence
- Continue from the nearest natural restart point
- Editor will cut out mistakes
- Do not apologize repeatedly — just restart

### Timing
- Target: 5 minutes
- Readthrough test: Time yourself once to check pacing
- If too long: Identify sections to trim. If too short: Pause more between sections.

## 7. Edit Plan

### Estimated Edit Time: 2–4 hours

### Steps

1. **Review takes** — Select the best full take or best segments
2. **Cut mistakes** — Remove pauses, restarts, mistakes
3. **Audio cleanup** — Normalize levels, remove background noise, add gentle compression
4. **Color grade** — Minor correction only. Warm, natural tones.
5. **Lower-third** — Add NDB-TV logo overlay (bottom-right, full duration)
6. **Intro** — Add intro animation (if available) or start cold
7. **B-roll inserts** — If using, place at designated points (see shot list)
8. **Text overlays** — Add program names during section 2 (optional)
9. **Outro** — Add end screen
10. **Audio mix** — Balance voice + music (if music used: very low, -25dB or quieter)
11. **Export** — H.264, 1080p, 30fps, AAC audio 192kbps
12. **Review** — Watch full video before sending for approval

### Final File Specs

| Parameter | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Format | MP4 |
| Codec | H.264 |
| Resolution | 1920×1080 |
| Frame rate | 30 fps |
| Audio | AAC, 192 kbps, 48 kHz |
| File size estimate | ~200–400 MB for 5 min at 8 Mbps |

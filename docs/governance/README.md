# Mission Protection

## Purpose

This document defines how NDB-TV protects its core mission from internal drift, external pressure, and short-term incentives that conflict with long-term purpose.

## Principles

1. **Mission over metrics** — Engagement, growth, and revenue serve the mission; they never override it.
2. **Public service first** — NDB-TV exists to serve Nouadhibou's citizens, not advertisers, sponsors, or political interests.
3. **Long-term orientation** — Decisions that trade mission integrity for short-term gain are rejected.
4. **Collective guardianship** — Every team member is a guardian of the mission, not just leadership.

## Safeguards

- Any decision that materially affects the mission requires editorial board review
- Content partnerships must pass a "mission alignment test" before approval
- Quarterly mission audit — review last quarter's content against mission criteria
- Whistleblower protection — any team member can raise mission concerns without fear

## Mission Drift Detection

| Signal | Severity | Action |
|--------|----------|--------|
| 3+ consecutive videos with low mission alignment | Warning | Editorial review |
| Sponsorship content exceeding 15% of weekly output | Warning | Rebalance schedule |
| Team member reports mission pressure | Immediate | Senior review within 48h |
| Outside organization attempts to direct content | Critical | Public transparency statement |

# Editorial Independence

## Purpose

NDB-TV maintains full editorial control over all content. No external entity — sponsor, partner, political organization, or individual — may dictate, influence, or veto content decisions.

## Commitments

1. **No paid placement** — Sponsors support the channel, they do not buy coverage.
2. **No political alignment** — NDB-TV serves the community, not any political party or movement.
3. **Transparent relationships** — All sponsorship and partnership arrangements are publicly disclosed.
4. **Right to refuse** — NDB-TV may decline any partnership or sponsorship for any reason.
5. **Disclosure in content** — Sponsored content is clearly labeled as such.

## Sponsor Relationship Boundaries

| Allowed | Not Allowed |
|---------|-------------|
| Sponsor mention in video description | Sponsor approval of content before publish |
| Logo on channel banner | Veto power over topics |
| General category sponsorship (e.g., "health content supported by X") | Script review or editing rights |
| Sponsor acknowledgment in outro | Requirement to produce positive coverage |

## Enforcement

- Editorial board reviews all sponsorship agreements
- Violations trigger immediate partnership review
- Pattern violations result in termination of the relationship

# AI Ethics Policy

## Purpose

As a channel that extensively covers artificial intelligence, NDB-TV must model the responsible AI use it advocates.

## Principles

### Transparency
- Always disclose when AI tools are used in content production
- Label AI-generated or AI-assisted content clearly
- Explain which tools were used and how

### Accuracy
- Verify AI-generated information before publishing
- AI is a research assistant, not a final authority
- Fact-check all AI outputs against reliable sources

### Human Accountability
- A human takes responsibility for every piece of content
- Automated content is not published without human review
- Error correction follows the same process as any content

### Empowerment, Not Replacement
- AI should augment human capability, not replace human judgment
- Content about AI should emphasize human agency
- Avoid framing that suggests AI makes humans obsolete

### Safety
- No promotion of harmful, deceptive, or unethical AI use
- No tutorials on bypassing AI safety measures
- Age-appropriate AI content for general audiences

## Disclosure Requirements

| AI Use Type | Disclosure Required |
|-------------|-------------------|
| AI-generated script draft | Yes — "Script assisted by [tool]" |
| AI-generated visuals/images | Yes — "Visuals generated by [tool]" |
| AI translation/subtitling | Yes — "Translated with [tool]" |
| AI voice/speech synthesis | Yes — "Voice generated by [tool]" |
| AI research assistance | Recommended |
| Standard editing tools (auto-caption, etc.) | Not required |

# Health Content Standards

## Purpose

Health content can inform, empower, and even save lives — but it can also mislead, harm, or create false hope. These standards ensure NDB-TV's health content meets the highest bar of responsibility.

## Requirements

### Content Standards

1. **Source-based** — Every factual claim must be traceable to a verifiable source
2. **Professional review** — Where possible, content is reviewed by a health professional
3. **Balanced presentation** — Acknowledge uncertainty, emerging research, and alternative views where relevant
4. **No individual medical advice** — Content addresses general health knowledge, never specific patient cases

### Required Disclaimers

Every health video must include (verbally or in description):

> "This content is for informational purposes only and does not constitute medical advice. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional for medical concerns."

### Prohibited Content

- Specific medication or treatment recommendations
- Dosage or administration instructions
- Diagnosis of conditions without professional involvement
- Promotion of unproven or pseudoscientific treatments
- Fear-based health messaging

### Quality Benchmarks

| Element | Standard |
|---------|----------|
| Source citations | Minimum 2 per major claim |
| Professional review | Required for treatment/prevention content |
| Date of information | Clearly stated |
| Updates to guidance | Corrected within 7 days of error discovery |

# Community Engagement Standards

## Purpose

NDB-TV exists in conversation with its community. These standards guide how we engage, listen, and respond.

## Commitments

1. **Active listening** — Comments, messages, and feedback shape content direction
2. **Respectful dialogue** — Engage with critics as seriously as supporters
3. **Timely response** — Community questions addressed within 48 hours where possible
4. **Transparency** — Clear about what feedback was acted on and why

## Comment Moderation

| Keep | Remove |
|------|--------|
| Constructive criticism | Hate speech |
| Corrections and fact-checks | Personal attacks |
| Content suggestions | Spam |
| Community dialogue | Misinformation |
| Questions | Harassment |

## Community Correspondent Network

Community correspondents are volunteers who:
- Contribute stories, footage, or interview access
- Represent NDB-TV in their neighborhoods and communities
- Receive training and support
- Are credited in content they contribute to

See `operations/volunteer-correspondents/` for management details.

# Governance Amendment Procedure

## Purpose

This document and the Founding Manual are living documents. This procedure governs how they are amended.

## Amendment Types

| Type | Approval | Effective |
|------|----------|-----------|
| Minor (typos, clarifications, formatting) | Any team member | Immediate |
| Standard (new content, policy updates) | Editorial board | 7 days after approval |
| Major (mission change, value change) | Editorial board + community consultation | 30 days after consultation |

## Process

1. **Proposal** — Written amendment proposal with rationale
2. **Review** — Editorial board review within 14 days
3. **Consultation** — For major changes, 14-day community feedback period
4. **Decision** — Board vote (simple majority for standard, 2/3 for major)
5. **Recording** — Amendment logged in document history

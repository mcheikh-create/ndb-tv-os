## Official Logo

### Logo Design

The NDB-TV logo is a professional news-style wordmark composed of:

- **Background:** Deep blue field — conveying trust, professionalism, and credibility
- **"NDB"** — White, bold typography — representing Nouadhibou with clarity and authority
- **"TV"** — Red, bold typography — the television/media identifier, using an accent color that signals energy and importance
- **Emblem/Icon:** AI microchip above an open book — symbolizing the fusion of artificial intelligence with knowledge and education. This icon sits above or beside the wordmark, anchoring the brand's dual identity as both a technology and educational media platform

**Visual Style:** Clean, modern television network appearance. Professional and trustworthy. Similar credibility level to major international media brands while maintaining strong local identity.

### Logo Usage

#### Primary Application
- **YouTube profile image** — Icon-only variant on deep blue
- **YouTube banner** — Horizontal variant
- **Video intros** — Logo animation (5s) at start of every video
- **Video outros** — Logo + end screen elements
- **Thumbnail templates** — Logo in bottom-right corner
- **Website header** — Primary variant
- **Facebook page branding** — Horizontal variant
- **WhatsApp channel branding** — Icon variant
- **Live broadcast overlays** — Semi-transparent in corner
- **Future NDB-TV mobile app** — Icon variant as app icon

#### Clear Space
- Minimum 1x logo height on all sides (measured from the tallest element)
- On busy backgrounds, increase clear space to 2x

#### Do's
- Use the official color variants only
- Maintain aspect ratio at all times
- Use on deep blue background (primary) or clean light background (light variant)
- Keep the AI chip + book icon visible and legible

#### Don'ts
- Do not stretch, distort, or rotate the logo
- Do not change colors — NDB is always white, TV is always red
- Do not add effects (shadows, outlines, gradients)
- Do not place on busy or low-contrast backgrounds
- Do not separate the wordmark from its background field
- Do not use unofficial color combinations
- Do not rearrange the NDB / TV typography relationship

### Secondary Colors

| Color | Name | Hex Code | Usage |
|-------|------|----------|-------|
| ██ | Navy | `#13294b` | Section backgrounds, depth |
| ██ | Light Blue | `#4a7dbb` | Links, secondary accents |
| ██ | Off-White | `#f0f2f5` | Light backgrounds |

### Color Usage Rules

- **Deep Blue** is the signature brand color — use as primary background for most applications
- **White** is for the "NDB" part of the logo and body text on dark backgrounds
- **Red** is reserved for the "TV" part of the logo and selective calls-to-action — do not overuse
- Secondary colors support but never replace the primary palette
- Minimum contrast ratio: 4.5:1 for text readability

## Typography

### Primary Typeface: Noto Sans Arabic
**Source:** Google Fonts (free)
**Usage:** All Arabic text — titles, body, subtitles, thumbnails

| Usage | Size | Weight | Letter Spacing |
|-------|------|--------|----------------|
| Channel name / hero | 64–96px | Bold (700) | -1px |
| Video titles | 40–56px | Bold (700) | -0.5px |
| Thumbnail text | 32–48px | ExtraBold (800) | Normal |
| Section headers | 28–36px | SemiBold (600) | Normal |
| Body text | 16–22px | Regular (400) | Normal |
| Captions | 14–18px | Regular (400) | Normal |
| Lower thirds | 24–32px | Bold (700) | Normal |

### Secondary Typeface: Inter
**Source:** Google Fonts (free)
**Usage:** English subtitles, technical terms, metadata

| Usage | Size | Weight |
|-------|------|--------|
| English subtitles | 14–18px | Medium (500) |
| Technical labels | 12–16px | Regular (400) |
| Metadata (tags, descriptions) | 11–14px | Regular (400) |

### Typography Rules
- Arabic is the primary language — all main text in Noto Sans Arabic
- English is secondary — use Inter for subtitles and technical content only
- Never use faux-bold or faux-italic — use the actual font weight
- On video thumbnails, text must be legible at mobile size (320px width minimum)

## Thumbnail Design System

### Elements (in order)
1. **Background** — Deep blue gradient or high-contrast scene image
2. **Subject** — Face visible, expressive, well-lit (if person-focused)
3. **Text overlay** — 3–7 Arabic words max, Noto Sans Arabic ExtraBold, white
4. **NDB-TV Logo** — Bottom-right corner, full-color primary variant
5. **Red accent** — A red element (line, shape, or underline) for energy

### Style Rules
- Deep blue color dominance
- Red used sparingly for emphasis only
- Text always white, never colored
- Maximum 1/3 of frame covered by text
- Legible at 320px width (mobile thumbnail size)
- Clean, minimal — news-network aesthetic
- No clutter, no excessive effects

### Thumbnail Templates
Created and maintained in `assets/thumbnails/`.

## Intro / Outro

### Intro Animation (5 seconds)
1. **0–1s** — Deep blue background, fade in
2. **1–3s** — AI chip + book icon animates into position
3. **3–4.5s** — "NDB-TV" wordmark appears (white NDB, red TV)
4. **4.5–5s** — Tagline appears below (optional): صوت المعرفة والابتكار في نواذيبو
5. **5s** — Full logo holds, transitions to content

**Audio:** Professional news-style stinger with a modern, tech-forward feel

### Outro End Screen (10 seconds)
1. **0–3s** — Final content moment fades
2. **3–10s** — End screen with:
   - NDB-TV logo (bottom-left or center)
   - 2 recommended video thumbnails
   - Subscribe button
   - Deep blue background

## Brand Voice Guidelines

### Tone
- **Professional** but not cold — credible like a news network, warm like a neighbor
- **Accessible** — explains complex topics in simple terms
- **Optimistic** — focused on opportunity and possibility
- **Respectful** — honors local culture, traditions, and values

### Language
- **Arabic (Hassaniya/Darija)** for conversational content — the voice of the street
- **Arabic (Modern Standard)** for formal content — the voice of authority
- **English subtitles** for AI and technology content — international reach

### Phrases to Use
- "المعرفة للجميع" — Knowledge for everyone
- "صوت المعرفة والابتكار" — The voice of knowledge and innovation
- "لا تحتكر ما تعلمته اليوم" — Do not hoard what you have learned today

### Phrases to Avoid
- Clickbait or sensational language
- Overly technical jargon without explanation
- Negative or fear-based framing
- Politically partisan language

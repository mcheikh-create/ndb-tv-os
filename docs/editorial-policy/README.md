# Editorial Policy

## Purpose

This document defines what NDB-TV publishes, what it does not publish, and the standards every piece of content must meet.

---

## Content Scope

NDB-TV produces content in the following categories:

1. **Educational** — Teaching practical skills and knowledge
2. **Informational** — News, updates, and awareness
3. **Inspirational** — Stories of achievement and possibility
4. **Entertainment** — Culturally appropriate entertainment with purpose
5. **Community** — Citizen voices, discussions, and participation

---

## Editorial Standards

### Accuracy
- All factual claims must be verified before publication
- Sources must be cited for technical, health, and scientific content
- Errors must be corrected promptly with clear acknowledgment of the correction
- Corrections to published content must be noted in the video description or pinned comment

### Fairness
- Multiple perspectives on contested issues where appropriate
- No misrepresentation of opposing views
- Interview subjects treated with respect regardless of agreement

### Independence
- Content is not for sale
- Sponsors do not influence editorial decisions
- Political content is limited to civic education, never partisan advocacy

---

## Prohibited Content

NDB-TV does not publish:

1. **Misinformation** — Knowingly false or misleading information
2. **Hate speech** — Content that attacks groups based on identity
3. **Dangerous acts** — Content that encourages harmful behavior
4. **Medical advice** — Specific treatment or medication recommendations
5. **Defamation** — False statements that harm individuals or organizations
6. **Copyright violations** — Unauthorized use of others' content
7. **Political partisanship** — Endorsement of or opposition to specific candidates or parties
8. **Exploitative content** — Content that exploits tragedy, suffering, or vulnerable people

---

## Language Standards

- **Primary:** Arabic (Modern Standard for formal, Hassaniya/Darija for conversational)
- **Subtitles:** English subtitles recommended for AI and technology content
- **Tone:** Respectful, informative, accessible — never condescending
- **Complexity:** Explain jargon, assume good faith but not prior knowledge

---

## Review Process

| Content Type | Review Required | Approver |
|-------------|----------------|----------|
| Standard episode | Self-review + checklist | Producer |
| Health content | Full review + source check | Editor |
| AI technical content | Technical accuracy check | Lead producer |
| Sensitive topics | Editorial board review | Board |
| Livestreams | Pre-broadcast checklist | Producer |
| Shorts | Self-review | Creator |

---

## Correction Policy

1. Errors discovered in published content must be corrected within 7 days
2. A correction notice must be added to the video description
3. Major errors require a pinned comment and community post
4. Persistent errors in a series trigger a process review

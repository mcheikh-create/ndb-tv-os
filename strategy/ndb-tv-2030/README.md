## The Five Platforms

NDB-TV 2030 envisions evolution from a single YouTube channel to five interconnected platforms, all under the NDB-TV brand and values.

### 1. Knowledge Platform

**Purpose:** Trusted, practical knowledge library for Nouadhibou.

**Evolution:**
- Year 1: YouTube channel with educational content
- Year 2: Searchable content library organized by topic
- Year 3: Website with categorized video library, written guides, downloadable resources
- Year 5: Community-contributed knowledge base, user-generated content

**Metrics:**
- 500+ hours of educational content
- 50,000+ monthly views
- 90%+ viewer satisfaction rate

### 2. AI Education Platform

**Purpose:** Making AI accessible and beneficial to every Mauritanian.

**Evolution:**
- Year 1: Zidni AI program on YouTube
- Year 2: AI workshop series (in-person and virtual)
- Year 3: Structured AI curriculum for schools and professionals
- Year 5: Certified AI literacy program

**Metrics:**
- 10,000+ AI literacy program participants
- Partnerships with 5+ educational institutions
- Recognized AI education provider

### 3. Health Awareness Platform

**Purpose:** Improving community health outcomes through information.

**Evolution:**
- Year 1: Health First program
- Year 2: Partnerships with clinics and health organizations
- Year 3: Health information hotline or WhatsApp service
- Year 5: Community health correspondent network

**Metrics:**
- Partnership with Ministry of Health
- Measurable health awareness improvement
- 100+ health episodes produced

### 4. Sports Development Platform

**Purpose:** Growing and celebrating local sports.

**Evolution:**
- Year 1: Sports coverage as part of main channel
- Year 2: Dedicated sports content track
- Year 3: Partnerships with sports federations
- Year 5: Youth sports development programs

**Metrics:**
- Coverage of 10+ local sports
- Partnerships with major local clubs
- Youth sports initiatives launched

### 5. Community Development Platform

**Purpose:** Civic engagement and citizen voices.

**Evolution:**
- Year 1: Voices of Nouadhibou and Community Dialogues
- Year 2: Community correspondent network formalized
- Year 3: Town hall series with measurable community impact
- Year 5: Catalyst for community-driven projects

**Metrics:**
- 50+ active community correspondents
- 10+ community projects influenced or initiated
- Recognized community development platform

## Enabling Factors

**To achieve this vision, NDB-TV needs:**
- Consistent content production team (2–5 people by Year 3)
- Stable funding (sponsorship + community support)
- Technology infrastructure (website, CMS, analytics)
- Community trust and engagement
- Strategic partnerships

## Risks to Vision

| Risk | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|--------|------------|
| Funding shortfall | Unable to scale | Multiple revenue streams, lean operations |
| Team burnout | Quality decline | Sustainable pace, volunteer network |
| Audience shifts platform | Loss of reach | Multi-platform strategy |
| Competition | Audience fragmentation | Unique local focus, community connection |
| Technology changes | Platform relevance | Stay current, adapt quickly |

# Health First (صحتك أولاً)

**Status:** Active
**Format:** Educational series (8–15 min) + Health Shorts
**Language:** Arabic / Hassaniya
**Frequency:** Bi-weekly

## Purpose

Improve community health outcomes through accessible, accurate health information in Arabic.

## Content Themes

- Preventive health and wellness
- Common health conditions explained
- Nutrition and healthy eating
- Maternal and child health
- Mental health awareness
- Local health services and resources

## Content Rules

All health content must comply with `docs/editorial-policy/` health content standards:

- Source every factual claim
- Include medical disclaimer
- No specific treatment recommendations
- Review by health professional where possible

## Target Audience

- Families seeking reliable health information
- Young adults building health awareness
- People who prefer health content in Arabic

## Episode Format

1. Hook — Why this matters to you
2. The science — What the evidence says (simplified)
3. Local context — How this applies in Nouadhibou/Mauritania
4. Practical steps — What viewers can actually do
5. Resources — Where to get help locally
6. Disclaimer

## Partnerships

Consider partnerships with:
- Local clinics and hospitals
- Ministry of Health
- WHO regional office
- Health-focused NGOs in Mauritania

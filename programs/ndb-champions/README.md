# NDB Champions

**Status:** Active
**Format:** Profile/interview series (8–12 min)
**Language:** Arabic / Hassaniya
**Frequency:** Bi-weekly

## Purpose

Celebrate local achievement and inspire others by profiling Nouadhibou's changemakers, innovators, and community leaders.

## Concept

Each episode profiles one person from Nouadhibou who is making a difference — in business, sports, education, health, arts, technology, or community service.

## Episode Format

1. Who they are — introduction to the person
2. Their story — how they started, challenges they overcame
3. Their work — what they do now (with footage)
4. Their advice — for the next generation
5. Community impact — how Nouadhibou is better because of them

## Profile Criteria

- Local to Nouadhibou (born, lives, or works here)
- Demonstrable positive impact on community
- Willing to share their story on camera
- Authentic and relatable

## Sourcing

- Community nominations (social media, correspondents)
- Local news and social media scanning
- Partner organizations
- Previous episode subjects' networks

## Production Note

- Interview format preferred over monologue
- Location shoots (their workplace/community) > studio
- Photo gallery at end for social sharing

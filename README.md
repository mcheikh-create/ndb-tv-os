# NDB-TV OS

> The operating system and institutional memory of NDB-TV.

**NDB-TV** is a digital media network dedicated to knowledge, opportunity, health, sports, community development, and artificial intelligence in Nouadhibou and Mauritania.

---

## Vision

A digitally fluent Nouadhibou where every citizen has access to practical knowledge, health awareness, economic opportunity, and responsible AI literacy — delivered through media that speaks their language and respects their culture.

## Mission

Help people improve their lives through practical knowledge and responsible use of technology.

## Operating Principle

**"لا تحتكر ما تعلمته اليوم"** — Do not hoard what you have learned today.

Knowledge should be shared and multiplied. Every skill acquired, every lesson learned, every insight gained carries an obligation to pass it on.

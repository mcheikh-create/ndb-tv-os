# Voices of Nouadhibou (أصوات انواذيبو)

**Status:** Active
**Format:** Street interviews / mini-documentary (5–8 min)
**Language:** Hassaniya / Arabic
**Frequency:** Weekly

## Purpose

Amplify citizen voices. Let the people of Nouadhibou speak about their city, their lives, their hopes, and their concerns.

## Format Types

### Street Series
- One question asked to multiple people
- Quick cuts, fast-paced
- 3–5 minutes

### Deep Dive
- One person, one story
- Longer format, more production
- 8–15 minutes

### Neighborhood Tour
- Explore a specific neighborhood
- Meet local characters, see local landmarks
- 10–20 minutes

## Content Guidelines

- No scripting — let people speak naturally
- Hassaniya preferred for authenticity
- Respect subjects' comfort levels
- Obtain clear consent for publication
- Represent diversity of Nouadhibou's communities

## Sourcing Subjects

- Public locations (market, port, university, sports fields)
- Community correspondent network
- Event attendees
- Social media volunteers

## Production Notes

- Mobile-friendly equipment (low profile, less intimidating)
- Natural lighting preferred
- Minimal interference with subjects' responses
- Release form required for all on-camera subjects

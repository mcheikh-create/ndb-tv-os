## 4. How Links Appear on YouTube

In YouTube Studio → Customization → About section:

**Links section format:**

| Label | URL |
|-------|-----|
| WhatsApp Channel | `https://whatsapp.com/channel/...` |
| Contact (Email) | `mailto:ndbtv@...` |
| [Future: Facebook] | `https://facebook.com/...` |

**Note:** YouTube allows up to 5 links in the About section. Use them wisely.

---

## 5. Safety Notes

- Do not put email address or phone number in the channel description text — use the dedicated Links section in YouTube Studio.
- Do not share personal phone numbers — use a dedicated email and WhatsApp channel.
- Do not promise response times.
- Set expectations: "We read every message but may not be able to reply to all."

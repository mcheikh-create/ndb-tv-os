# Zidni AI (زدني)

**Status:** Active
**Format:** Educational series (10–20 min) + Shorts
**Language:** Arabic / Hassaniya
**Frequency:** Weekly

## Purpose

"Zidni" means "increase me" — a reference to the Quranic prayer "رب زدني علما" (My Lord, increase me in knowledge). This program makes AI literacy accessible to Arabic-speaking audiences in Nouadhibou and beyond.

## Content Themes

- AI tools and practical applications
- AI news in Arabic
- How-to guides (ChatGPT, image generators, coding assistants)
- AI ethics and responsible use
- Local AI applications for Mauritania

## Target Audience

- Arabic-speaking professionals and students
- People curious about AI but intimidated by technical content
- Small business owners who could use AI tools

## Sample Episode Ideas

1. "ما هو الذكاء الاصطناعي؟" — AI explained in simple terms
2. "كيف تستخدم ChatGPT في عملك اليومي" — ChatGPT for daily work
3. "أدوات AI مجانية يجب أن تعرفها" — Free AI tools you must know
4. "الذكاء الاصطناعي والأخلاق" — AI and ethics
5. "AI يساعد في التعليم" — AI in education

## Format

- Host on camera or voiceover with screen recordings
- Real demos rather than theoretical explanations
- Downloadable summaries/resources in video description

## Production Notes

- English subtitles recommended for broader reach
- Tools should be demonstrated, not just described
- Assume no prior AI knowledge

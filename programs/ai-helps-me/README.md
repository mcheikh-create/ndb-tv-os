# AI Helps Me

**Status:** Active
**Format:** Documentary-style (5–10 min)
**Language:** Arabic / Hassaniya (English subtitles)
**Frequency:** Bi-weekly

## Purpose

Show real people using AI to solve real problems. This program makes AI tangible by focusing on use cases, not theory.

## Concept

Each episode features one person — a student, teacher, doctor, business owner, artist, or professional — demonstrating how they use AI tools in their daily work or life.

## Format

1. Meet the person — Who they are, what they do
2. The problem — What challenge they faced
3. The AI solution — How they used AI (real demo)
4. The result — What changed
5. Their advice — For viewers who want to try

## Content Guidelines

- Authentic stories only — no scripted "customer testimonials"
- Subjects must actually use the tools they demonstrate
- Level of AI skill shown can be beginner, intermediate, or advanced
- Focus on practical benefit, not technical sophistication

## Sourcing Subjects

- Community correspondent network
- Local businesses and professionals
- AI workshop participants
- Social media outreach

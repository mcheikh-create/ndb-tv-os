# NDB-TV Episode Delivery Rules

## Script Adherence

1. The approved script is the final word. Do not add, remove, or rephrase without approval.
2. If you discover an error in the script during recording, stop, note it, and inform the editor.
3. Do not improvise opinions, jokes, or commentary not in the script.

## Pacing

| Content Type | Target Duration | Words (Arabic) |
|-------------|----------------|----------------|
| Short (YouTube Shorts/Reels) | 30–60 seconds | 60–120 words |
| News Brief | 2–3 minutes | 250–400 words |
| Educational Explainer | 5–8 minutes | 600–1,000 words |
| In-depth Feature | 10–15 minutes | 1,200–2,000 words |

## Recording Etiquette

- Record in a quiet, echo-free environment.
- Warm up your voice before recording (2 minutes).
- Drink water — avoid milk or syrupy drinks before recording.
- Leave 2 seconds of silence at start and end for editing.
- Clap at the start of each take (creates a visible audio spike for sync).

## Multiple Takes

- First take: Read through for flow.
- Second take: Correct major mistakes.
- Third take (if needed): Focus on tone and energy.
- Best take wins — editor selects.

## After Recording

- Upload raw footage to `production/presenters/[presenter-name]/raw/`
- Complete self-review checklist:
  - [ ] Audio clean and clear
  - [ ] Framing consistent throughout
  - [ ] No background distractions
  - [ ] Script followed accurately
  - [ ] NDB-TV branding visible

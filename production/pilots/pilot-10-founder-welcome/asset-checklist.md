## Asset Details

### Intro Animation
- **Content:** 5-second NDB-TV logo reveal on deep blue background
- **Style:** Clean, professional news-style
- **Audio:** Short brand sting/logo sound
- **Fallback:** Start video without intro if not available

### Outro End Screen
- **Content:** 10-second end screen with NDB-TV logo + subscribe button + 2 video slots
- **Style:** Deep blue background, consistent with branding
- **Pattern:** Available as YouTube end screen template

### Thumbnail
- **Design:** Founder photo (medium shot) on deep blue background. NDB-TV logo bottom-right.
- **Text:** "مرحباً بكم في NDB-TV" — bold, white, Noto Sans Arabic
- **Size:** 1280×720px
- **Style guidelines:** See `docs/branding/README.md` → Thumbnail Design System

### Background Music
- **Genre:** Ambient, light, positive
- **Volume:** Low — must not compete with voice
- **Source:** Must be rights-cleared. Options:
  - YouTube Audio Library (free)
  - Free Music Archive (CC-licensed)
  - Uppbeat (free tier)
  - Original composition
- **Do NOT use:** Unlicensed music from any source

### Subtitles/Captions
- **Primary:** Arabic (Hassaniya script) — for hearing accessibility and clarity
- **Secondary:** French subtitles — for non-Hassaniya speaking viewers
- **English:** For internal review
- **Format:** SRT files
- **Timing:** Keyed to final edited video

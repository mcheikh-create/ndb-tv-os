# I Love NDB (أنا أحب انواذيبو)

**Status:** Active
**Format:** City pride series (3–8 min)
**Language:** Arabic / Hassaniya
**Frequency:** Weekly shorts + Monthly long-form

## Purpose

Celebrate Nouadhibou — its people, places, history, culture, and beauty. Build a sense of pride and belonging among residents and diaspora.

## Content Themes

- **Landmarks** — Cap Blanc, Port, La Plage, markets, neighborhoods
- **Culture** — Music, food, traditions, festivals
- **History** — Stories from older residents, historical photos
- **People** — The characters that make the city special
- **Change** — Development, new projects, positive transformation
- **Diaspora** — Nouadhibouis abroad, their memories and connection

## Format

- Shorts: Single striking image/scene + caption, 15–60 seconds
- Long-form: Themed compilation, 5–15 minutes
- Community-sourced: Viewer-submitted photos and videos

## Content Guidelines

- Positive framing without ignoring reality
- Authentic representation, not tourist-style
- Credit community contributors
- Encourage viewer submissions

## Engagement

- Use hashtag #أنا_أحب_انواذيبو
- Monthly photo contest
- Viewer submission features in episodes

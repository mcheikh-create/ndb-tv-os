## English Internal Notes

NDB-TV is a community media and learning network based in Nouadhibou, Mauritania. Content is primarily in Hassaniya Arabic.

Focus areas: education, useful information, culture, youth skills, local stories, community participation.

Presenters: Community members (human). AI presenters may also be used for educational and informational content — always clearly labeled.

Core principle: Accuracy first. All content is reviewed for accuracy and community benefit.

No political statements. No claims of official authority. No medical diagnoses (health content is educational/prevention only).

## Sponsor Relations

### Onboarding
1. Welcome email with agreement summary
2. Deliverable timeline
3. Assets collection (logo, description, links)

### Ongoing
- Monthly check-in during active sponsorship
- Deliverable tracking
- Analytics sharing

### Renewal
- Contact 30 days before end of term
- Present results and options for next term
- Negotiate terms if changing

# NDB-TV — AI Presenter Disclosure Policy

**Status:** Draft — awaiting founder approval
**Scope:** All NDB-TV video content featuring AI-generated presenters

---

## 1. Purpose

NDB-TV may use AI-generated presenters/avatars for educational, informational, and tutorial content. This policy ensures viewers are never misled about whether they are watching a real person. Transparency is mandatory.

---

## 2. Core Rules

| # | Rule | Details |
|---|------|---------|
| **2.1** | **Always disclose** | Every video with an AI presenter must clearly state this fact. No exceptions. |
| **2.2** | **Disclosure in title** | The video title must include one of: "AI Presenter" / "مقدم رقمي" / "Présentateur IA" |
| **2.3** | **Disclosure on screen** | The first 5 seconds of the video must show a text overlay: "هذا المقدم رقمي — AI Presenter" on a deep blue background. |
| **2.4** | **Disclosure in description** | The video description's first line must state: "هذا الفيديو يقدمه مقدم رقمي (AI). This video features an AI presenter." |
| **2.5** | **No impersonation** | The AI presenter must not be designed to look like or sound like any specific real person without their written consent. |
| **2.6** | **No fake guests** | AI presenters must not be presented as real guests, experts, or community members. |
| **2.7** | **No synthetic voice misrepresentation** | If using AI voice, it must not be presented as a real person's voice. |
| **2.8** | **Founder pre-approval** | The first AI presenter design and first AI-presenter video must be approved by founder before publishing. |

---

## 3. Disclosure Locations

### Video Title
```
[Topic] — AI Presenter | مقدم رقمي
Example: "ما هو الذكاء الاصطناعي؟ — AI Presenter | مقدم رقمي"
```

### Video Description (First Line)
```
هذا الفيديو يقدمه مقدم رقمي (AI). This video features an AI presenter.
```

### On-Screen (First 5 Seconds)
```
هذا المقدم رقمي
AI Presenter
```
Text on deep blue (#0a1f3d) background. White text, medium size. NDB-TV logo bottom-right.

### On-Screen (Throughout Video)
Lower-third label appears intermittently:
```
AI Presenter — مقدم رقمي
```
Subtle, in bottom-right corner, 50% opacity, appearing every 60 seconds for 10 seconds.

## 4. Content Limits for AI Presenters

| Allowed | Not Allowed |
|---------|-------------|
| Educational content (AI, tech, skills) | Breaking news / current events |
| Public service announcements | Political commentary |
| Tutorials and how-to guides | Medical advice or diagnosis |
| Cultural stories (with human review) | Personal opinion or editorial |
| Translated content (education) | Community dialogue or interviews |
| Structured learning series | Sensitive or controversial topics |

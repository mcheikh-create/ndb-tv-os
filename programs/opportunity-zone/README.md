# Opportunity Zone (منطقة الفرص)

**Status:** Active
**Format:** Informational series (5–10 min)
**Language:** Arabic / Hassaniya
**Frequency:** Weekly

## Purpose

Connect Nouadhibou residents with economic opportunities — jobs, training, funding, and business development resources.

## Content Types

1. **Opportunity Spotlight** — Specific job, training, or funding opportunity
2. **How to Apply** — Step-by-step application guides
3. **Success Stories** — People who found opportunities through the program
4. **Resource Guides** — Overviews of available support (government programs, NGOs, training centers)

## Content Sourcing

- Online job portals and training platforms
- Government announcements
- NGO program launches
- Local business hiring announcements
- Community correspondent tips

## Accuracy Standards

- Verify all opportunity details before publishing
- Include application deadlines, requirements, and contact information
- Update or remove expired opportunities
- Clearly state "This information is current as of [date]"

## Episode Format

1. The opportunity — What it is
2. Who it's for — Eligibility
3. How to access — Step-by-step
4. Deadline — When to apply by
5. Links — Description with resources

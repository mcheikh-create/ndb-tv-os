# Volunteer Correspondents

## Purpose

The community correspondent network extends NDB-TV's reach, brings authentic local voices, and creates a pipeline for community-sourced content.

---

## What is a Community Correspondent?

A volunteer who:
- Contributes stories, footage, or interview access from their community
- Represents NDB-TV in their neighborhood or area of expertise
- Receives training and support from the NDB-TV team
- Is credited in content they contribute to

---

## Becoming a Correspondent

### Requirements
- Resident of Nouadhibou
- Access to a smartphone with camera
- Willingness to learn basic filming techniques
- Alignment with NDB-TV values
- No prior media experience required

### Application Process
1. Expression of interest (form, WhatsApp, or in-person)
2. Brief conversation with NDB-TV producer
3. Orientation session (30 min)
4. First assignment with support
5. Ongoing relationship

---

## Correspondent Roles

### Story Spotter
- Identify interesting stories, people, and events
- Report tips to NDB-TV team
- No filming required

### Field Contributor
- Film footage and conduct basic interviews
- Submit raw footage to NDB-TV for editing
- Basic smartphone filming training provided

### Community Host
- Host segments or interviews in their area
- On-camera role
- Training and support provided

---

## Correspondent Guidelines

- Always identify yourself as an NDB-TV correspondent
- Obtain verbal consent before filming anyone
- Respect privacy and cultural norms
- Report stories accurately without embellishment
- Never pay for stories or interviews
- Submit content within 48 hours of capturing

## Support Provided

- Basic filming training (smartphone)
- NDB-TV branded merchandise (optional)
- Credit in all content contributed to
- Invitation to community events
- Reference letter for volunteers (after 6 months)

## Correspondent Network Management

- Maintain contact list in `operations/volunteer-correspondents/roster.md`
- Monthly check-in with active correspondents
- Quarterly training session
- Annual appreciation event

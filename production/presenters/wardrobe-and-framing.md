# NDB-TV Presenter Wardrobe and Framing

## Wardrobe

### General Rules
- **Solid colors preferred.** Avoid busy patterns that cause moiré on camera.
- **NDB-TV brand colors:** Deep blue, white, and red are encouraged.
- **Modest and professional.** Respect local cultural norms.
- **Avoid:** All-black (absorbs light, flattens face), all-white (blows out), thin stripes (camera flicker), large logos.

### Recommended
- Solid blue, navy, or white top.
- For formal content: Collared shirt or modest blouse.
- For casual/youth content: Clean, neat casual wear.
- Minimal jewelry — avoid pieces that catch light and create reflections.

### Not Allowed
- Clothing with political, religious, or offensive messaging.
- Hats or sunglasses (unless culturally required).
- Unlicensed branded merchandise.

## Framing

### Standard Shot
- **Medium shot:** Head to mid-torso. Hands visible when gesturing.
- **Eye level:** Camera at eye level or slightly above.
- **Headroom:** Small gap above head — not too much, not cropped.
- **Look space:** If looking slightly off-camera (for interview format), leave space in the direction they're looking.

### Composition
- **Center:** Presenter centered or slightly right (left space for text/visuals on left).
- **Background:** Clean, uncluttered. Branded backdrop if available.
- **Lighting:** Key light in front (45° to camera), fill light on other side, backlight to separate from background.

## Technical Minimums
- Resolution: 1080p minimum
- Frame rate: 30fps
- Lighting: Well-lit face — no shadows across eyes
- Audio: Clean, no background noise, consistent levels

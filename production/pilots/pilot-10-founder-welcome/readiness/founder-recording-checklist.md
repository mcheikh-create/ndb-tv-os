# NDB-TV Pilot #10 — Founder Recording Checklist

**One page. Complete before recording starts.**

---

## Preparation (Day Before)

| # | Item | Done? |
|---|------|-------|
| 1 | Script re-read at least twice — know the flow, not memorize | ☐ |
| 2 | Outfit selected — solid color, no busy patterns, clean | ☐ |
| 3 | Filming location checked — quiet, no echo, no interruptions | ☐ |
| 4 | Phone / camera charged to 100% | ☐ |
| 5 | Enough free storage space (estimate: 2 GB for 10 min of 1080p) | ☐ |
| 6 | Backup recording device available (second phone) | ☐ |

---

## Setup (30 Minutes Before Recording)

| # | Item | Done? |
|---|------|-------|
| 7 | Camera set up — on tripod or stable surface, eye level | ☐ |
| 8 | Framing checked — medium shot (head to mid-torso) | ☐ |
| 9 | Lighting set up — key light at 45°, face well-lit, no shadows | ☐ |
| 10 | Background checked — clean, no clutter, no windows behind | ☐ |
| 11 | Microphone clipped on — lavalier or external mic | ☐ |
| 12 | Audio test: record 10 seconds, play back — clear? No hiss? | ☐ |
| 13 | Lighting test: record 10 seconds — face visible? | ☐ |
| 14 | Glass of water within reach but out of frame | ☐ |
| 15 | Phone on silent, door closed, pets/kids settled | ☐ |
| 16 | Script placed near camera (can glance if needed) | ☐ |

---

## Recording

| # | Item | Done? |
|---|------|-------|
| 17 | **Test clip:** Record 30 seconds of opening — check audio + video quality | ☐ |
| 18 | **Take 1:** Record full script straight through. Don't stop for small mistakes. | ☐ |
| 19 | **Take 2:** Pause 30 seconds. Record full script again. Relax. | ☐ |
| 20 | **Take 3 (optional):** If Takes 1–2 both have major issues, try once more. | ☐ |
| 21 | **Close-up retake (optional):** If needed, record just the closing 30 seconds again. | ☐ |
| 22 | **Save files:** Name clearly — `pilot10_take1.mp4`, `pilot10_take2.mp4` | ☐ |
| 23 | **Backup:** Copy files to second device or cloud before wrapping up | ☐ |

---

## During Recording — Quick Reminders

- ➡ Look at the **camera lens**, not the screen
- ➡ Speak at a natural pace — slightly slower than conversation
- ➡ Pause between sections — let each point land
- ➡ Smile naturally when greeting and closing
- ➡ If you stumble, pause, take a breath, restart the sentence
- ➡ Don't apologize — just restart

# Strategy: First 1,000 Subscribers

**Prerequisite:** First 100 subscribers reached.
**Status:** Pending
**Target Completion:**

---

## Objective

Reach 1,000 genuine subscribers — the threshold for YouTube Partner Program monetization (shorts) and a credible media presence.

## Why 1,000 Matters

- YouTube Partner Program (Shorts monetization) at 1,000 subs + 10M Shorts views OR 1,000 subs + 4,000 watch hours
- Credibility with potential partners and sponsors
- Algorithm begins treating the channel as established
- Community is large enough for meaningful engagement

## Strategy Pillars

### 1. Content Scaling (60%)
- Increase to 2 core videos + 4 shorts per week
- Introduce a weekly livestream as anchor event
- Launch the second program (rotate focus)
- Guest appearances and collaborations

### 2. Distribution Scaling (20%)
- Build WhatsApp broadcast list to 500+ subscribers
- Launch Instagram and TikTok channels (short-form adaptation)
- Cross-promote with complementary channels
- Community correspondent contributed content

### 3. Community Building (10%)
- Weekly community post with engagement question
- Viewer shoutouts and featured comments
- Polls to choose upcoming topics
- Virtual meetup at 500 subscribers

### 4. Quality Improvement (10%)
- Upgrade production as budget allows
- Invest in better audio equipment
- Develop consistent visual branding
- Improve thumbnail design system

## Risks

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|------------|
| Burnout from scaling content | Maintain quality-over-quantity lens |

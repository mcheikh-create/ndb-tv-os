## Objective

Reach 100 genuine subscribers who actively watch and engage.

## Why 100 Matters

100 subscribers is the first proof of concept. It shows:
- Content is resonating with real people
- The value proposition is clear
- Basic discoverability is working
- Friends and family sharing organically

## Tactics

### Content (85%)
1. **Publish consistently** — 1 core video + 2 shorts per week minimum
2. **Focus on Zidni AI** — Most shareable, most searchable topic
3. **Make every video shareable** — One quotable moment, one practical takeaway
4. **End every video with subscribe CTA** — Explicit, verbal, on-screen

### Distribution (10%)
1. **WhatsApp broadcasts** — Share to 3+ relevant groups per video
2. **Facebook cross-posting** — NDB-TV page shares every upload
3. **Personal network** — Each team member shares to their contacts
4. **Community correspondents share** — In their networks

### Community (5%)
1. **Respond to every comment** — Within 24 hours
2. **Ask viewers to subscribe** — In community posts
3. **Thank first 100** — Special acknowledgment video at milestone

## Metrics to Watch

- Views per video (target: 100+ in first week)
- Watch time (target: 50%+ average retention)
- Subscriber conversion rate (target: 5%+ of viewers)
- Shares per video (target: 5+ in first 48h)

## Post-Milestone

Once 100 subscribers is reached, begin First 1000 strategy.

## Detailed Shot Plan

### Shot 1: Intro (0:00–0:05)
**Type:** Animation/graphic
**Content:** NDB-TV intro — logo animation on deep blue background
**Audio:** Intro music sting
**Fallback:** If no intro animation exists, start directly with Shot 2

### Shot 2: Opening (0:05–0:30)
**Type:** Medium shot — Founder
**Framing:** Head to mid-torso. Centered. Eye level.
**Content:** "Salam alaikum. Ana [name]." Introduction, direct address.
**Background:** Clean, simple — plain wall with NDB-TV logo sign, or bookshelf, or consistent color
**Lighting:** Soft key light (45° left), fill light (45° right), backlight to separate from background
**Audio:** Founder's voice, clean microphone
**Notes:** NDB-TV logo as lower-third overlay throughout

### Shot 3: What NDB-TV Is (0:30–1:30)
**Type:** Medium shot — Founder
**Framing:** Same as Shot 2. Subtle changes OK (small head movement, hand gestures).
**Content:** Explanation of NDB-TV programs and vision.
**Visual overlay:** Consider text overlays for program names: زيدني AI, الفرصة, إيحب نواذيبو
**Notes:** Maintain natural hand gestures. Do not rush.

### Shot 4: How We Work (1:30–2:30)
**Type:** Medium shot + optional B-roll
**Framing:** Same or slightly wider.
**Content:** Human + AI presenter explanation.
**Visual:** At 1:45, optional 5-second B-roll of someone working at a computer (generic, no identifiable person). Return to founder.
**Notes:** Key moment: establish AI transparency rule.

### Shot 5: Why We're Building This (2:30–3:30)
**Type:** Medium shot — slightly tighter
**Framing:** Head to chest. Creates more intimate feel.
**Content:** Personal motivation. Local context.
**Visuals:** Consider B-roll of Nouadhibou port, street, or community scene (10 seconds at 2:45). Must be rights-cleared footage.
**Notes:** Most personal section. Tone should be sincere, not performative.

### Shot 6: Call for Participation (3:30–4:15)
**Type:** Medium shot — Founder
**Framing:** Return to standard medium shot.
**Content:** Enumeration of who should participate.
**Visual:** Text overlay for contact method (WhatsApp number or email — TBD, add later).
**Notes:** Clear, direct. Eye contact. Pause between each group addressed.

### Shot 7: Closing (4:15–5:00)
**Type:** Medium shot — Founder
**Content:** Thanks, call to subscribe, sign-off.
**Notes:** Hold final smile for 2 seconds before cut to outro.

### Shot 8: Outro (5:00–5:10)
**Type:** Graphic/end screen
**Content:** NDB-TV logo, subscribe button, 2 recommended video slots (future episodes)
**Audio:** Outro music sting

## Post-Production Notes

- Color grade: warm natural tones. Do not over-process.
- Audio: consistent level throughout. No sudden volume changes.
- Transitions: simple cross dissolves between main shots. No flashy effects.
- Lower-third: NDB-TV logo (bottom-right). Founder name (bottom-left) if desired.
- Subtitles: Arabic subtitles (Hassaniya script) recommended for accessibility.

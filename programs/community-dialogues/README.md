# Community Dialogues (حوارات مجتمعية)

**Status:** Active
**Format:** Discussion / Town Hall (30–60 min)
**Language:** Arabic / Hassaniya
**Frequency:** Monthly

## Purpose

Create a structured space for community conversation on issues that matter to Nouadhibou. Not debate — dialogue. Not polarization — understanding.

## Format Types

### Expert Panel
- 2–4 experts on a topic
- Moderated discussion with audience questions
- 45–60 minutes

### Town Hall
- Open participation format
- Community members share perspectives
- 60 minutes

### One-on-One Interview
- Deep conversation with one informed voice
- 20–30 minutes

## Topic Selection Criteria

- Relevant to Nouadhibou community
- Multiple legitimate perspectives exist
- Constructive dialogue is possible
- Topic affects a significant portion of the community

## Moderation Principles

- Moderator is neutral, not adversarial
- Every participant gets space to speak
- Factual corrections offered respectfully
- Goal is understanding, not agreement
- No个人 attacks permitted

## Topics (Candidates)

- Youth unemployment and entrepreneurship
- City development and infrastructure
- Education quality and access
- Healthcare availability
- Technology and the future of work in Nouadhibou
- Environmental concerns (fishing industry, pollution)
- Sports development
- Cultural preservation

## Partnership Opportunities

- Local universities and schools
- Community organizations
- Professional associations
- Local government (for civic issues)

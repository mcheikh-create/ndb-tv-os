# NDB-TV Pilot #10 — Thumbnail Text Options

## Design Rules for Thumbnail Text

- **Maximum 5 words** in Arabic (longer text becomes invisible on mobile)
- **Bold weight** — Noto Sans Arabic Bold
- **White** (#ffffff) on deep blue (#0a1f3d) — high contrast
- **No exclamation marks, no hype language**
- **No political or exaggerated claims**
- **Community-oriented** — welcoming, not self-congratulatory

---

## Option 1: Direct Welcome (Recommended)

**Arabic:** مرحباً بكم في NDB-TV
**Translation:** Welcome to NDB-TV
**Words:** 4
**Tone:** Warm, direct, inviting
**Note:** Most straightforward. Establishes channel name immediately. Works at mobile size.

## Option 2: Vision Statement

**Arabic:** رؤيتنا لنواذيبو
**Translation:** Our Vision for Nouadhibou
**Words:** 3
**Tone:** Aspiring, community-focused
**Note:** Shortest option. Names the city directly. Good for local identity.

## Option 3: Origin Story

**Arabic:** NDB-TV تبدأ من نواذيبو
**Translation:** NDB-TV Starts from Nouadhibou
**Words:** 4
**Tone:** Grounded, local pride
**Note:** Emphasizes local roots. Slightly longer but still mobile-readable.

## Option 4: Mission Focus

**Arabic:** منصة للمعرفة والمجتمع
**Translation:** A Platform for Knowledge and Community
**Words:** 4
**Tone:** Educational, inclusive
**Note:** Highlights the dual focus (knowledge + community). Good for setting expectations.

## Option 5: Tagline (Official)

**Arabic:** صوت المعرفة والابتكار
**Translation:** The Voice of Knowledge and Innovation
**Words:** 4
**Tone:** Professional, aspirational
**Note:** Uses the approved v1.0 tagline. Most professional option.

---

## Recommendation

**Primary:** Option 1 — "مرحباً بكم في NDB-TV"

**Reasoning:**
- It is the most direct and welcoming — appropriate for a first video
- It includes the channel name (brand recognition)
- It is short enough for mobile readability (4 words)
- It is non-political and non-hype
- It pairs naturally with the subtitle or video title context

**Secondary text (on thumbnail, optional):** 
Smaller line below: رؤيتنا لنواذيبو (Option 2)
Thus: "مرحباً بكم في NDB-TV — رؤيتنا لنواذيبو"
This would be 2 lines, primary + subtitle.
